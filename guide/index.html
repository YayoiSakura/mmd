Season II

Api-Miku Remodelings


(2018~)






Innocent, Lively and Lovely Api-Miku


Yayoi Sakura’s MMD

Season II CONCEPT

  • To try various costumes on Api-Miku (Appearance Miku).
  • Hand maid feeling, like a cosutume playing salesgirl at the Comic Market.

Season II FEATURE

  • resizing body proportions
  • expanding the mesh area until the very limit
  • details (described later)

Feature -1. resizing body proportions

  • total balance： bit younger than original model
  • legs： shortened



Feature -2. expanding mesh area

In the new version, it’s enabled to responde with various costumes, such as ‘Shimakaze from Kan-Kore’, ‘Cherry boy killer’, etc.



Cherry boy killer……



Feature -3. adding shaping and refining on details

parts detail
shoulder・arms add curve
breast flattening
body ribs up, shaping abdomen muscle, navel up, shrinking pelvis
back shaping scapula, shaping the line of the back bone
hips shaping tailbone, tightened
legs naturalize the line of legs from gom boots like shape and flat foot

What I couldn’t write here, it’s done in its own way……

  • adhere to the form of the original model.

Reflection points on Season I

Reflection point -1
  • Legs are unnaturally long
  • Roughly UV unfolding



Reflection point -2

The total proportion of the old model seemd too much elderly. In SeasonII, I arranged the proportion to responde to its face look.




Season I（old models）, Season Iβ（proportion changed）, Season I / II (50%/50%)

Reflection point -3

The old model had a somothing sagging derriere. In SeasonII, it has been tightened.




Season I（old ver）, Season IIβ（trial ver）, Season I / II (50%/50%)

Policy on Season II



keeping a flavor of the old model as much as possible,

  • make corresponde “face look/age” with “body look/age” in order to increase a range of selection on cosutumes.
  • refine details as well
