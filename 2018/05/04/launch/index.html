How to get

You could find model data (.zip) at “bowlroll”, de-facto standard MMD uploading site.

You would also find the DOWNLOAD CARD(.png image) at Patreon or Fantia (patreon like site in japan). The CARD would shows you the passwaord to D/L.

In general, only subscribed members can see the CARD with password.

  • It’s a ‘Free plan’ or ‘Free cource’, so please feel free.
